Our Story

KarmaHQ was born from frustration. Our founders were running Reddit marketing campaigns manually — buying karma accounts, managing IPs, warm-up periods — and it was exhausting.

When Reddit's traffic exploded from 60M to 1.1B monthly visitors, and AI models started citing Reddit as their #1 source, we knew the opportunity was massive.

So we built KarmaHQ — the software that makes all of this easy, scalable, and authentic. Today, we help 50+ brands tap into Reddit's full potential.
